Test Cases - Multiple Access Protocols
======================================

Test Case 1: Low Traffic Load
- 4 PCs sending slowly (1 packet every 3-4 seconds)
- Observed collision rate for each protocol

Test Case 2: High Traffic Load
- 4 PCs sending packets rapidly and simultaneously
- Measured frequency of collisions

Test Case 3: Pure vs Slotted ALOHA
- Random transmission vs timed/slot-based transmission
- Compared number of collisions

Test Case 4: CSMA/CD Collision Handling
- Rapid transmission to force collisions
- Observed detection and back-off behavior

Test Case 5: CSMA/CA Avoidance Mechanism
- Wireless topology with multiple PCs
- Observed RTS/CTS handshake and low collision rate

Each test repeated 3 times for consistency.
Error/collision simulated by rapid simultaneous transmissions.
